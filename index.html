Fencing Statistics Analysis App

I collaborated with a teammate to develop an app using MATLAB aimed at analyzing and comparing fencing statistics from 2014-2021. The goals of the project were:

  • Allow users to analyze and compare fencing statistics from 2014-2021 via an app interface
  • Provide users with a variety of means to analyze fencing statistics, such as visualizations, statistical summaries, and customizable filters.
